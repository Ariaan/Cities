One of the most diversified cities in America is Honolulu.There are many beaches formed around Honolulu. It is the capital of Hawaii. It is known to be the largest city in the state of Hawaii. In Honolulu, the official languages are English and Hawaiian.

Some interesting facts about Honolulu is that Spam is popular in this city. In Honolulu it is illegal to feed sharks. "Hono" means sheltered and "lulu" means bay and thats where Honolulu city gets it's city name from. Honolulu simpily means sheltered bay.
