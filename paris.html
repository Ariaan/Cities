Paris

One of the most visited cities in the world is Paris. A landmark that people often think about when they think of Paris is the Eiffel Tower.

Paris is also known as the "City of Light". In France it is one of the most populated cities. One of their main sports is soccer. Paris has alot of museums and landmarks for people to visit. It is also known to be a "City of Fasion". This city has many places to shop and bakeries for people to get treats like macaroons.
